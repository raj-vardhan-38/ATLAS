Revolutionizing Biodiversity Science

ATLAS harnesses the power of environmental DNA (eDNA) to unlock the secrets of our planet's biodiversity. Our AI-driven platform analyzes genetic material from water, soil, and air samples to create comprehensive ecosystem maps.

From tracking endangered species to discovering new life forms, we're transforming how scientists, conservationists, and policymakers understand and protect our natural world.

How It Works

Our streamlined process transforms environmental samples into actionable biodiversity insights

1. Sample Collection

Collect water, soil, or air samples from any environment using our standardized protocols

2. DNA Sequencing

Extract and sequence environmental DNA using next-generation sequencing technology

3. AI Analysis

Our machine learning algorithms identify species and analyze biodiversity patterns

4. Data Visualization

Interactive dashboards and reports provide actionable insights for conservation
